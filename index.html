Why I joined Coding Temple

To fully understand why I chose to attended Coding Temple, you’ll have to learn a little bit about me. My name is Christopher Oberdick but I go by Mike. I am 26 years old and live with my girlfriend, our five cats, and dog in a small town near Weirton, West Virginia. Currently I work for a local construction company as an estimator for commercial buildings. For the past year or so we have decided that we do not want to stay in this area anymore, so I started to look into different career choices that would open us up to the chance of moving. I took a 2 week crash corse in coding and loved it. I found my spending hours doing research and reading on different coding jobs and opportunities. I came to the decision to attened Coding Temple after talking to the amazing admissions team and learning about how in depth the program is. I really felt like I could set myself up for the future the best coming out of Coding Temple over any other bootcamp. I am super excited for this opportunity and can’t wait to see how much this will help me in the future.
